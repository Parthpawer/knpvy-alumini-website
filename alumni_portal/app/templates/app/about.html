Our History

From a small cohort of passionate graduates to a vibrant, global network.

Founding Story

Established to strengthen the bond between alumni and the institute, the association began as a volunteer‑led initiative. Over the years, it has evolved into a structured body supporting student welfare, research, and community outreach.

“Alma mater in our hearts, excellence in our actions.”

What We Do

We organize reunions and annual meets, fund scholarships and assistantships, power the placement cell with industry connects, and celebrate the achievements of our illustrious alumni across the globe.
