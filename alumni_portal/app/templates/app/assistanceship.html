Eligibility & Checklist

Who Can Apply
  • Full‑time KNPCVS students (as per call notice)
  • Good academic standing; attendance as per rules
  • Availability: 8–12 hours/week during term
  • Basic lab safety (for RA) or pedagogy orientation (for TA)
Documents
  • Updated CV (1 page)
  • Latest marksheet
  • Faculty referral (where required)
  • Brief statement of interest (max 150 words)
