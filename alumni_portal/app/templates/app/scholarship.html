Eligibility & Criteria

General Requirements
  • Full‑time KNPCVS students (as per category)
  • Good academic standing; no disciplinary action
  • Supporting documents (marksheets, income proofs)
  • Recommendation(s) where applicable
Document Checklist
  • Filled application (PDF)
  • Marksheets (latest semester)
  • Income certificate (for Aid)
  • Brief CV / Statement of Purpose (if asked)

How to Apply

Read Guidelines
Check eligibility, documents, and deadlines for your category.
Prepare Documents
Marksheets, income certificate (for Aid), and CV/SOP if asked.
Download Application
Use the latest PDF from the Downloads section.
Submit as per Notice
Follow the department notice for submission mode (office/email).
Review & Result
Committee evaluates and publishes results on the notice board/site.
Notes
  • No fees are charged at any stage.
  • Incomplete applications may be deferred to the next cycle.
  • Conflicts of interest must be declared by recommenders.
  • Assistantships require monthly work logs endorsed by faculty.
